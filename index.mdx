## What is Ragou?

Ragou is an intelligent AI chat platform that transforms how your customers interact with your website. Using advanced Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) technology, Ragou provides instant, accurate answers to customer questions while offering seamless fallback to human support when needed.

With simple 2-line CDN integration, powerful analytics, and enterprise-grade security features, Ragou empowers businesses to deliver exceptional customer experiences while gaining valuable insights into customer needs.
