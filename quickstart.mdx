## Get Ragou running in four simple steps

Follow this guide to integrate Ragou's intelligent AI chat into your website and start helping your customers instantly.

### Step 1: Create your Ragou account

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="user-plus" title="Sign up for Ragou">
    Create your free Ragou account to get started:
    
    - **New user?** [Sign up here](https://app.ragou.net/auth/sign-up)
    - **Already have an account?** [Login here](https://app.ragou.net/auth/login)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="users" title="Create your first team">
    All projects must live within a team, so let's create one:
    
    1. After logging in, create your first team
    2. Choose a unique team slug (this cannot be changed later)
    3. Complete the team setup process
    
    <Note>The team slug must be unique across all Ragou users.</Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 2: Set up your project

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="folder-plus" title="Create your first project">
    1. Navigate to **Projects** in your dashboard
    2. Make sure your newly created team is selected
    3. Click **Create Project** and fill in your project details
    4. Save your project settings
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="key" title="Get your Project ID">
    You'll need your Project ID for the chat widget integration:
    
    1. Go to [Projects Dashboard](https://app.ragou.net/home/teams/projects)
    2. Click on your desired project
    3. Copy your **Project ID** from the project dashboard
    
    <Tip>Keep this Project ID handy - you'll need it in the next step!</Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 3: Integrate the chat widget

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="code" title="Add the script tag">
    Add this script tag to your HTML `<head>` or `<body>` section, above where you'll place the chat widget:
    
    ```html
    <script src="https://chat-widget-cdn.devon-henry09.workers.dev/app.umd.js"></script>
    ```
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="comments" title="Add the chat widget">
    Place the chat widget element where you want it to appear on your page:
    
    ```html
    <chat-widget title="Ragou" projectId="your-project-id"></chat-widget>
    ```
    
    Replace `your-project-id` with the Project ID you copied in Step 2.
    
    <Warning>Make sure to replace `your-project-id` with your actual Project ID!</Warning>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 4: Upload your data

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="upload" title="Upload your documents">
    Your AI chatbot needs data to provide intelligent answers:
    
    1. Go to [Data Management](https://app.ragou.net/home/teams/data)
    2. Select your project from the dropdown
    3. Upload your documents (supported formats: `.md`, `.txt`, `.docx`)
    4. Click **Upload to AI** to process your documents
    
    <Tip>Upload comprehensive documentation for better AI responses!</Tip>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="flask" title="Test your chatbot">
    Verify everything is working correctly:
    
    1. Visit the [Chat Playground](https://app.ragou.net/home/teams/chat)
    2. Select your project
    3. Ask test questions to confirm your data is loaded
    4. Head back to your website and try the chat widget!
    
    <Note>It may take a few minutes for uploaded documents to be processed and available.</Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
